Sacred Festivals

Experience the vibrant celebrations that honor centuries-old traditions

February 2025

Losar - Tibetan New Year

The most significant festival celebrating the Tibetan New Year with traditional dances, prayers, and colorful decorations.

May 2025

Buddha Purnima

Celebrating the birth, enlightenment, and death of Lord Buddha with special prayers and processions.

June-July 2025

Hemis Festival

A spectacular monastery festival featuring masked dances and traditional music celebrating Guru Padmasambhava.

October 2025

Dashain Festival

A major Hindu festival celebrating the victory of good over evil with family gatherings and traditional ceremonies.

Sacred Monasteries

Journey through ancient centers of spirituality and learning

Rumtek Monastery

The Dharmachakra Centre, one of the most significant monasteries of the Kagyu school of Tibetan Buddhism.

Enchey Monastery

An ancient monastery belonging to the Nyingma order, known for its beautiful architecture and peaceful atmosphere.

Pemayangtse Monastery

One of the oldest and premier monasteries of Sikkim, offering breathtaking views of the Kanchenjunga range.
